About the Developer

Kaitlan Oliphant

Hello! I'm Kaitlan Oliphant, a passionate front-end developer with a love for creating beautiful and functional web applications. I am based out of Orlando, Florida. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, user-friendly solutions.

I have two bachelor's degrees that I obtained in college. First, I received a Media Arts and Science degree with a concentration in Web Development from Indiana University - Indianapolis (formaly known as Indiana University - Purdue University Indianapolis). Within this degree I learned a little bit of the different technologies out in the world that we use everyday. I got to dip my toes in 3D animation, photo-editing, game design, and more. Being able to go through all the different tracks that this degree offered, I knew from the beginning that I wanted to focus on web development and UX design.

My second degree, is a bachelor's degree from Bellevue University in Software Engineering/Developement. This degree helped me realize that Front-End developement was my passion. I learned a little of different back-end languages with this degree, such as Java, C#, and Python. I also learned about databases, algorithms, and data structures. This degree helped me understand the full stack of web development, but my heart lies in the front-end.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, hanging out at the theme parks, or relaxing at home with my husband and our fur babies.
